Who We Are

Bird Dog Capital is a private equity firm that hunts for opportunities and builds category-defining businesses.

We're about precision, patience, and trust — finding what's hidden, signaling opportunity, and acting decisively only at the right moment.

We invest with focus, discipline, and insight, partnering with management teams to identify overlooked opportunities, drive operational excellence, and create sustainable value for all stakeholders.

How We're Different

Our philosophy underlies our differentiated approach to creating value.

Risk-Adjusted Returns Matter

We focus on creating and monetizing competitive advantages in every transaction in order to maximize returns and minimize risk

Industry Focus is Critical

Combining Financial and Intellectual Capital is Powerful

We are investors and consultants. When able, we engage as consultants to build value enhancement plans side-by-side with owners and then make a far more informed investment

Performance Speaks Louder than Promises

We pay for performance, not promises, by aligning interests and implementing performance-based compensation plans for owners, key managers and even our own firm

Plan for the Unexpected

Integrity and Character Mean Something

It is not enough to work with smart, experienced people. We seek to partner with people of high integrity and character...people we can be proud to be associated with. Our reputation is at stake and so is yours
